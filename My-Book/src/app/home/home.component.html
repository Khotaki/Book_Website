The Hidden Realm

A Journey Beyond the Veil

Book Summary

In a world where shadows whisper and light deceives, Elara discovers a hidden realm existing parallel to our own. "The Hidden Realm" follows her journey as she unravels the mysteries of her ancestry and the ancient war that threatens to spill over into the human world.

Guided by cryptic visions and an unlikely alliance with a rogue shadow-walker, Elara must navigate a landscape of shifting loyalties and dangerous magic. As the barrier between worlds weakens, she realizes that the key to saving both realms lies unlocked within her own forgotten memories.
